An Asynchronous World

March - May 2021

This is a zine about NYU student experiences during COVID lockdown and remote learning.

Contribution: Graphic Design, Digital Art
Technologies: Figma, Autodesk Sketchbook

View it here!

I collaborated with Jay Li Quek, the president of Tech@NYU, to create this zine. We wanted to help students feel connected during remote learning and give them a platform through which to share their experiences and creativity. The zine includes ten student art & writing submissions, one interview, and six graphics I created from surveying students. I led the art direction and graphic design.
