Albert Redesign

April 2022

This is a personal project I worked on to imagine how the course search website for NYU students could be improved.

Contribution: User Research, UI/UX Design
Technologies: Figma

Current Albert Demo Redesign Demo

NYU Albert is the portal where NYU students can find and register for courses as well as track degree progress. Many NYU students share the sentiment that Albert is confusing and difficult to navigate, making the already stressful task of finding and registering for your courses even more challenging. So, I thought it would be interesting to do a redesign of the site and try to improve its information hierarchy and navigation efficiency.

This was also my first time experimenting with product design, which I've always been interested in trying out. Although I had used Figma to make graphics before, I had never used it to prototype, so it was really cool learning about features like components and auto layout, and I'm excited to build on these skills in the future!
