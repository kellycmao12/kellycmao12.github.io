Infinity Mirrors

Dec 2021

This is an interactive VR museum experience inspired by Yayoi Kusama's Infinity Mirrors.

Contribution: Web Development
Technologies: JavaScript, p5.js, A-Frame VR library

Try it out!

Yayoi Kusama is known for her iconic Infinity Mirror rooms, where mirrors on all sides reflect dozens of lights, sculptures, and even the viewer's own reflection endlessly. I created my own interpretation of Infinity Mirrors to study the possibilities for utilizing VR to augment museum and art-viewing experiences, in order to make them more participatory and accessible. During the experience, the user navigates using the arrow keys and can enter each of three rooms to explore and activate the mirrored walls.
