About Me

I’m from the SF Bay Area and currently live in NYC. I just graduated from NYU Tisch School of the Arts with a double-major in Computer Science and Interactive Media Arts.

I’ve always been captivated by the power of design and loved exploring new mediums of creating things. Whether it’s building out a full-stack website or prototyping an app I dreamed up in Figma, I’m passionate about building exciting ideas and embracing an interdisciplinary approach to my work. I’m most interested in product & experience design, and incorporating technology and interactivity into projects to make them more compelling, accessible, and delightful.
